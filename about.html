About



stahlstadtatelier is an interdisciplinary design studio founded in linz, austria, by paul hehenberger, felix mader and hannes wedel. the studio works across the fields of architecture and product design, focusing on the creation of refined spatial and physical objects. paul and hannes met while studying architecture at tu vienna, while felix and paul have known each other since their shared hometown of linz. united by a common interest in material, structure and experimentation, stahlstadtatelier operates at the intersection of architectural thinking and object-basedÂ design.
